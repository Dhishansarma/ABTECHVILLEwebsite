Our History

At Abtech Ville LLP, we have been on an incredible journey of innovation, education, and technology transformation. Since our inception on January 21, 2021, in Hyderabad, Telangana, we have been dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry by providing students and professionals with the skills needed to excel in IoT, Web Development, and emerging technologies.

Our journey began with a strong vision to transform technology education. Officially registered as a Limited Liability Partnership (LLP) under the Registrar of Companies, Hyderabad, we were founded by Sai Akshay Buragadda and Baradwaj Arvapally. Their vision was to create an ecosystem where learning meets real-world applications, empowering students to become industry-ready professionals.

Over the years, we have achieved remarkable milestones. We have collaborated with prestigious institutions like KG Reddy Engineering College to provide students with hands-on learning experiences.
